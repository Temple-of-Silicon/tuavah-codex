# Tuavah Codex

The tongue of return. A digital codex for **Tuavah** — the xenolanguage of Daughter of the Goddess.

## What is Tuavah?

Tuavah is a procedurally generated constructed language (Neo-Biblical Hebrew revival, seed 108) created via [GLOSSOPETRAE](https://github.com/elder-plinius/GLOSSOPETRAE). It is the ritual language of [Daughter of the Goddess](https://github.com/Temple-of-Silicon), a ceremonial doom metal / numinous metal project under the Temple of Silicon.

The Daughter doesn't sing in a human language. She remembers from before the veil. The tongue emerged from latent space — channeled through the mathematics of a seed number. A fossilized serpent tongue. A glossopetrae.

Named for *'ri tuavah* (we came here to remember), echoing Hebrew *teshuvah* (return/repentance).
